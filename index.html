About Me






Hi, my name is Jared Simpson. I am from Powell, Ohio and went to Bishop Watterson High School. I graduated from The Ohio State University's college of Education and Human Ecology in 2017 with a specialization in Consumer and Family Financial Services. Since college, I worked in the facility services industry for about 2 years before leaving to focus on the pursuit of becoming a web developer.

In my personal life, I live in Grandview, Ohio and have two cavapoos named Kate and Pippa. I enjoy biology, studying languages, Ohio State Buckeyes football and basketball, am fond of traveling and am an avid stock trader in my free time.
