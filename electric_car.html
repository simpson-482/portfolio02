Electric Cars

Jared Simpson
Research Essay
November 19, 2011

Electronic vehicles are the next step in tackling many problems facing Americans today. These issues include the economy, the war on terror, rising gas prices and the environment. These are problems that concern everyday Americans like you and me and the electric car is a possible solution to all of them. Electric cars are a seemingly no-brainer to the advancement of American culture as they are more energy efficient, would decrease American dependency on foreign oil, and are more beneficial to the environment than internal combustion engines. The electric car has developed into models that cost less for the consumer, and are more stylish in appearance then previous models. Although many people state that electricity must be made through the burning of coal, and the damage to the environment would be equivalent to that of burning gasoline; America as a nation is more able to develop the renewable resources at our disposal through the use of wind energy, geothermal energy, hydrogen energy, ocean energy, and bioenergy. As American society and the societies of the world are becoming more technologically advanced, the time to make the switch from the internal combustion engines of the past to the electric models of the future is now.
